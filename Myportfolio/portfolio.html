Project 1

This HTML webpage is designed to display a collection of mathematical and economics formulas in a user-friendly format, utilizing Bootstrap for styling and responsiveness. It features a fixed navigation bar at the top for easy access to different sections, which include topics such as geometry (area and perimeter of shapes), interest calculations, quadratic equations, matrix operations, HCF/LCM, tax computations, and depreciation rates.
